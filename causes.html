Things I Care About

Causes that drive my work and life

Gender Equality

I am passionate about promoting gender equality through my writing and art. My works often feature strong female protagonists who challenge societal norms and expectations. I believe in creating stories that empower readers of all genders to pursue their dreams without limitations.

Animal Rights

I am particularly concerned about the welfare of animals in slaughterhouses, especially chickens and pigs. Through my work, I aim to raise awareness about their conditions and advocate for more humane treatment. I believe in using storytelling as a tool to inspire compassion and promote positive change in how we treat these animals.
