Hi. I'm Situ E. Chen.

I am an author and graphic novelist, currently in progress.

Learn about what I do

Last updated: August 15, 2025

Here's all the stuff I do.

I write and illustrate.

I write books

about people finding their own way and chasing their dreams. I have experimented with many genres/formats so far, like realistic fiction, historical fiction and prose. I am currently working on another realistic novel about a striving writer. So far I have published 2 novels in total.

I create graphic novels

exploring concepts of love, dreams and self-discovery. I enjoy using subtle colors and gentle symbolism. Although all three of my currently published graphic novels were drawn by hand, I am learning to draw digitally.

I also have a lot of other hobbies

like watching mystery movies, reading fiction novels, creating my own videos and writing book/movie reviews. I also have a gray poodle named Tedo, and I'm very proud of her!

I care very deeply about gender equality and slaughterhouse animal rights. I especially advocate for the rights of slaughterhouse-born chickens and pigs, and I hope to spread that message using my work.
