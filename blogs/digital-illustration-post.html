My Journey into Digital Illustration

Posted on April 15, 2024

As I experience with new styles and forms of art, one important journey is my transition from tradional hand-drawing to Ipad digital drawing. I hope that by learning this new form of art, the visual impact of my stories will be improved.

However, this journey came with callenges. I started by taking digital creation classes, and soon began to experiment with brushes myself, sometimes with inspiration from the web. For now I have found my prefered brush (Studio pen) and am adapting to this new kind of creation.

I am very proud to have created Emily's Diary 2 with digital artwork! If you want any suggestions or advice, I would recommend searching for sources online or simply experimenting with types of painting.
